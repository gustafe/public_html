GDPR Privacy Notice for gerikson.com

This is a private site, all content is created by me, Gustaf Erikson.

No data is being collected from anyone who visits this site, apart from what can be gleaned from the HTTP access logs.

No data is shared to third parties.

Access log data is used to determine whether access to this site is being abused (i.e. via DDoS attack).

Data is stored for the duration of 10 access log rotations - at the time of writing this is around one week.

I will not remove any data from the logs before it is rotated out.

Complaints and questions can be raised via the details in my contact page.
